GOT OCD?

When discussing minecraft I am aware that ocd is an amazing texture pack by the even more amazing disco, but mostly the term ocd stands for the well known uncontrollable need to achieve perfection. Even in places where no-one will ever see. That’s the server’s ambition in a nutshell. Hence the name.

Now YOU are that player that squeezes every last drop of power out of your machine to run that new shadermod or HD texture pack, or tries to get better at – let’s say – landscaping. If you recognize yourself as this type of player you are more than welcome to check out the server and help it become AWESOME.

OCD SKILL TREES

Basic

Everyone first joins the server as a Newbie. After registration to our forum, Member rank is obtained. Members have access to most worlds and get two free pets; Ocelot and Wolf. After a week of playing on the server players automatically become Veteran. Veterans get one new chicken pet; pet ride and pet hat permissions aswell as colored signs and Headgrabber. Every other tree can be started once a Veteran.

Survivor

The path of a Survivor on OCD Server starts pretty easy. You start out in Feralchild. You will find villages, shops, commands, and staff to help u out on your quests. Then if u have proven yourself very worthy; you will be invited to play in our Hermit Survivalworld. This comes real close to vanilla survival. No staff-help. No shops. No commands. Just your friends and you making awesome machines and villages that will definately make even the best creative builders jealous. Hermits recieve an [H] addition.

Architect

Becoming an Architect is easy for some and almost impossible to others. If true talent is spotted, first there is Builder rank. This means you get to build in OCD Server’s Vipworld and get tips and lots of help improving yourself. If you’re in need of practice; Builder rank is the way to learn alot about building. If you’re a rounded builder already Architect rank is not far away. Players that achieve Architect rank get World Edit, and Plotme Admin. Architects are expected to here and there help out other players with their WE- and Plot-powers.

Engineer

Redstone kings can become an Engineer on OCD Server. The rank of Engineer is obiviously the hardest rank to achieve. Not only must there be flawless original redstone content shown to OCD Server’s Engineers, but most importantly a relation of trust must be built up with the servers staff. Engineers recieve World Edit and Plotme Admin permissions. Engineers are expected to here and there help out other players with their WE- and Plot-powers.

Donator

The OCD Server is all about fun. Thats why we have extremely generous yet very cheap Donation packs. They vary from setting ur nickname and chatcolor to a pack that contains all pets and so much more. Be sure to check out the Donate! part of the site for more on our Donation packs. Thanks to donations we can maintain the quality level you are used to from OCD Server!

Staff

Some players have a natural talent for helping others out. If these actions are genuine you might catch the eye of one of our staff. If so you might be invited to become an HMOD. This is a trial mod rank. From there you either go back to your old rank or you are promoted to a full MOD. Proven help to the server over an extended period of time is rewarded with an Admin rank. Permissions for each rank are explained to you ingame when received. One BIG TIP: Never ask to be staff, or any chance of becoming one is immediatly removed.
